Adobo Recipe


Description

Adobo is a classic Filipino dish made by simmering meat in a flavorful mixture of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices, creating a rich and savory taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken or pork (cut into serving pieces)
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ½ cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Cooked rice (for serving)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté the minced garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken or pork and brown all sides.
  3. Pour in soy sauce, vinegar, and water, then add black peppercorns and bay leaves.
  4. Bring to a boil, then lower the heat and let it simmer for about 30 minutes.
  5. Stir occasionally and check if the meat is tender.
  6. Optional: Add sugar to balance the flavors.
  7. Let it simmer until the sauce thickens slightly.
  8. Remove bay leaves and turn off the heat.
  9. Serve with steamed rice and enjoy!
