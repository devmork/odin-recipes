Burger Recipe


Description

A classic burger recipe featuring a juicy, seasoned beef patty grilled to perfection, topped with fresh lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and your favorite sauce, all served on a toasted bun for the ultimate flavor experience. üçîüî•

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb ground beef (80/20 blend for juiciness)
  • Salt & pepper (to taste)
  • 1 burger bun (toasted)
  • 1 slice cheese (cheddar, American, or your choice)
  • 1 lettuce leaf (fresh & crisp)
  • 1-2 slices tomato (ripe & juicy)
  • 1-2 slices onion (red or white, optional)
  • Pickles (optional, for tanginess)
  • Condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayo, or burger sauce)
  • Butter (for toasting the bun)

Steps

  1. Prepare the patty: Mix ground beef with salt and pepper, then shape into a round patty.
  2. Cook the patty: Grill or pan-fry over medium-high heat until browned on both sides (about 3-4 minutes per side).
  3. Melt the cheese: Place a slice of cheese on top of the patty in the last minute of cooking.
  4. Toast the bun: Lightly butter and toast the bun on the pan or grill until golden.
  5. Assemble the burger: Place lettuce, tomato, onions, and pickles on the bottom bun. Add the patty with melted cheese on top.
  6. Add condiments: Spread ketchup, mustard, mayo, or your favorite sauce on the top bun.
  7. Serve and enjoy! Close the burger, press gently, and dig in! üçîüî•
