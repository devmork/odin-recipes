Pizza Recipe


Description

A delicious homemade pizza with a crispy golden crust, rich tomato sauce, gooey melted cheese, and a variety of fresh toppings—perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ½ cup tomato sauce
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder

Steps

  1. Mix warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead until smooth.
  3. Cover and let the dough rest for about an hour, or until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  5. Roll the dough onto a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  6. Spread tomato sauce evenly across the dough.
  7. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  8. Add your favorite toppings.
  9. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
  10. Slice and enjoy your homemade pizza!
